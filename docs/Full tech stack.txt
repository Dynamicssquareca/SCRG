Seeding             Knex.js seeds       -         Initial data (admin user, etc.)
Connection Pool     pg + pg-pool        8.x       Efficient DB connection pooling

Why PostgreSQL?
- Robust JSONB support for storing flexible metadata
- Excellent date/time handling for month-based filtering
- Strong indexing for fast queries on large case datasets
- Battle-tested, open-source, free
